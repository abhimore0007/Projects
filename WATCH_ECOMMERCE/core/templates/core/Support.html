Frequently Asked Questions

To set the time on your watch, pull the crown out to the second position, then turn it clockwise or counterclockwise to adjust the time. Push the crown back in to set it.

To replace the battery, unscrew the back of the watch using a special tool. Remove the old battery and replace it with a new one. Make sure the watch is sealed tightly to prevent water damage.

Check the charging cable and ensure it is properly connected. If the problem persists, try cleaning the charging contacts on both the watch and the charging cable.

To clean your watch, use a soft cloth and a mild soap solution. Avoid submerging the watch in water unless it's waterproof. Dry it thoroughly with a clean cloth after cleaning.
